Learn more about the Ride to Conquer Cancer

The Ride to Conquer Cancer® is a cycling journey, a fundraising crusade and your chance to BE EPIC.

Over two days and 200 kilometres, we will take to the majestic Canadian countryside in solidarity and promise to show that cancer is no match against our conviction to conquer it.

We provide support for your Epic Ride:

  • Year-round Ride Guide assistance
  • Informative orientation sessions
  • Comprehensive online Participant Centre
  • Frequent local training rides

The money you raise through The Ride directly supports local cancer patients and their families, so join our epic community now and register for The Ride today!
