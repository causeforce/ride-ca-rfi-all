Learn more about The Ride to Conquer Cancer

The Ride to Conquer Cancer is an incredible 2-day cycling challenge throughout Canada's most dynamic landscapes.

Join thousands of Riders of all abilities for an epic fully-supported experience you'll never forget.

Be the difference as we unite to Conquer Cancer!

The money you raise funds cancer research and care throughout Canada.

We provide support for your Epic Ride:

  • Coaching and training tips
  • Training rides in your area
  • A personal webpage for fundraising
  • Informative Orientation sessions
